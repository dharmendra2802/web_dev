Here's a timeline of Neymar Jr

  • February 1990 - Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born 5 February 1992), known as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer
  • May 1, 2003 - During the spring of 2003 Neymar was spotted by Santos FC at a training clinic in Brazil
  • May 7, 2009 - At 17 years of age, Neymar played his first professional game for Santos FC against Mogi Mirim
  • January 1, 2010 - Neymar completed his first season with Santos scoring a booming 42 goals in 60 games
  • April 10, 2011 - Before the summer of 2011 Brazilian national team head coach Luiz Felipe Scolari offered Neymar a spot on the team
  • September 20, 2011 - Neymar won the South American Footballer of the Year award over the likes of Ronaldinho and Robinho
  • January 1, 2012 - While Santos was attempting to try to keep Neymar in Brazil, the brazilian was offered a new contract by his club reaching over 55 million dollars
  • June 1, 2012 - During the summer of 2012, Neymar scored his 100th goal in his career and at Santos FC
  • May 27, 2013 - After numerous deals falling through with other clubs, the president of Barcelona FC annonced the signing of Neymar from Santos FC
  • June 1, 2013 - The Brazilian team went on the win the Confederation cup over Spain in the final and Neymar was awarded the golden boot at the end of the tournament for being the MVP of the tournament.
  • October, 2014 - At the age of just 22 years, Neymar had scored 40 goals in 58 internationals, and had become the fifth highest goalscorer for his national team
  • August 27, 2017 - FC Barcelona filed a lawsuit against Neymar, demanding he return the contract renewal bonus he received as well as €8.5 million in damages and an additional 10% for the arrears
  • April 2, 2017 - Neymar scored his 100th goal for Barcelona in his 177th appearance for the club, netting in a 4–1 win over Granada
  • August 3, 2017 - Barcelona announced that Neymar's legal representatives made a payment of €222 million to the club, equal to the release clause of his contract, which constituted the most expensive transfer ever
  • August 12, 2018 - Neymar scored PSG's opening league goal of the 2018–19 season in a 3–0 win over Caen at Parc des Princes
  • June 5, 2019 - He suffered an ankle injury in a 2–0 friendly win over Qatar and was ruled out of the tournament
  • October 10, 2019 - Neymar played his 100th match for Brazil in a friendly draw 1–1 with Senegal in Singapore
  • September 2, 2020 - It was reported that Neymar, along with PSG teammates Ángel Di María and Leandro Paredes, tested positive for COVID-19
  • August 10, 2012 - Neymar's former Barcelona teammate Lionel Messi signed for PSG
  • September 9, 2021 - Neymar scored the second goal in a 2–0 win over Peru and became Brazil's all-time top scorer in FIFA World Cup qualification matches, with 12 in total
